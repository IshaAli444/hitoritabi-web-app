Why We Write

Words are small journeys. We write to map the interior towns we visit privately — to leave notes on benches for future selves and strangers. Hitoritabi is a shared room where any writer may place those notes.

  • • To remember the places that taught us to feel.
  • • To carve soft bridges between readers and strangers.
  • • To practice noticing the small, stubborn details.
